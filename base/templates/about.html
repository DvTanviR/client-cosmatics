Who We Are

ZhuHai Beyond Cosmetics Co., Ltd is a leading cosmetics OEM/ODM manufacturer since 2006, based in Zhuhai, Guangdong, China. Our strategic location near Hong Kong and Macao enhances our global reach and ensures efficient connectivity with customers worldwide.

All of our products comply with EU and FDA regulations. Thanks to our high-quality standards, our products are well recognized in the market. Over the past few years, we have worked closely with more than 100 customers across 30+ countries.

What We Make

Our experienced R&D team specializes in a wide range of high-quality cosmetic products. We offer eyeshadows in various colors and finishes, blushes for a natural glow, and lip glosses for a glossy finish. Our lipsticks come in a range of shades, from nudes to vibrant reds, to suit every preference. Each item is carefully made to meet the highest standards of quality and performance.

How We Make

Our experienced R&D team develops a wide range of cosmetic formulations. We cover cosmetics for lips, eyes, and face. Throughout production, we perform in-house tests on raw materials to finished goods, assessing pH, viscosity, melting points, stability, and microbiological standards.
